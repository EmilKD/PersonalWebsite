About Me

I love physics-based simulations! I've received my B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineernig from K.N. Toosi University of Technology where I had the pleasure of researching on real-time deformation methods under Dr. Ali Nahvi's supervision. My focus was computational mechanics, but during my studies, my childhood passion for computer games and game developement cultivated and I started self-studying CGI and computer graphics. I am also an avid rock climber who's always searching for new opertunities of outdoor sport climbing and bouldering
