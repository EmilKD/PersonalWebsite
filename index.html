About Me

I am a computer graphics, interactive simulations, and game development enthusiast. I've studied Mechanical Engineernig at K.N. Toosi University of Technology where I had the pleasure of working on real-time deformation methods under Dr. Ali Nahvi's supervision. My interest in computer graphics was first sparked in childhood by playing around with map editors (which are unfortunately less common these days). In high school, I began learning CGI and visual effects, but gradually I realized that I was more interested in what happens “under the hood”. This led me to self-study core concepts in computer science, computer graphics, and game development.

I started my professional journey as a freelance effects artist for a year after which I joined NasirSimulator where I've been working on developing driving simulators and virtual environments with Unreal Engine 5.


I am also an avid rock climber, constantly seeking new opportunities for outdoor sport climbing and bouldering. If you can't find me, I am probably hanging from some rock. Also I love playing the guitar, you can find some of my original music here.
